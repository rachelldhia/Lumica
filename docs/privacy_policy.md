# Privacy Policy for Lumica

**Last Updated:** December 15, 2024

## Overview

Lumica ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

---

## Information We Collect

### Account Information

- Email address (for authentication)
- Display name
- Profile photo (optional)

### App Data

- **Mood entries**: Mood selections with timestamps
- **Journal entries**: Notes you create (stored encrypted)
- **Chat history**: Conversations with Lumi AI

### Automatically Collected

- Device type and OS version
- App usage analytics (anonymous)
- Location (only if you grant permission, for mood insights)

---

## How We Use Your Data

| Data Type | Purpose |
|-----------|---------|
| Email | Account authentication, password reset |
| Mood entries | Display mood charts, personalize insights |
| Journal entries | Store and sync across devices |
| Chat history | Provide context-aware AI responses |
| Location | Location-based mood pattern insights (optional) |

---

## Third-Party Services

We use the following services:

- **Supabase**: Secure database and authentication
- **Google Gemini AI**: Powers Lumi chat (conversations are processed but not stored by Google)

---

## Data Security

- All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest
- We use industry-standard security practices
- You can delete your account and all data at any time

---

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access your personal data
- Request data deletion
- Export your data
- Opt out of analytics
